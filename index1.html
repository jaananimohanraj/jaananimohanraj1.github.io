Chocolate Bars

Caramel Chocolates

Firstly, What is caramel? Caramel is a medium to dark-orange confectionery product made by heating a variety of sugars. It can be used as a flavoring in puddings and desserts, as a filling in bonbons, or as a topping for ice cream and custard. Caramel is often incorporated into chocolates to give it a chewy sort of feel. This is good especially when the chocolates also have hard or crispy ingredients. Giving it a crispy, yet chewy feel. There are many different chocolate bars with caramel in them. Some examples of this are: caramilk, twix, snickers, milky way, galazy, mars, oh henry!, Mr.Big, Skor,etc. There are also different type of caramel, one widely known version is salted caramel.

Nut Chocolates

There are many different types of nut chocolates. There are chocolate bars with nuts in them and there are nuts with chocolate around them. There are also nut flavoured chocolate. Chocolate covered nuts are very common, you will often find chocolate covered almonds and chocolate covered hazelnuts at stores. These can be for chocolate lovers that are trying ot be more healthy or nut lovers that want a bit of sweet in their lives. Chocolate covered nuts are a favourite all around. Chocolates with nuts in them and with nut flavours are even more common. One of the most found nuts in chocolate is hazelnut. I'm sure you are all familiar with the all time favourite Ferrero Rocher. This has a hazelnut crumb mixed with chocolate, hard shell, a creamy milk chocolate inside with a hazelnut in the middle. Even the other flavoured variations of this all time favourite have hazelnuts on the inside.
